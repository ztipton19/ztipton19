👋 Hello!

I'm a data scientist and ecologist with a unique interdisciplinary background spanning biology, environmental science, and economic analytics. Currently pursuing my second master's degree in Economic Analytics at the University of Arkansas, I specialize in applying rigorous statistical methods and computational tools to solve real-world problems.

🎯 What I Do

My work sits at the intersection of ecology, economics, and data science. I combine statistical modeling, spatial analysis, econometric methods, and database management to extract insights from complex datasets. Whether analyzing stream ecosystems, economic trends, or land-use patterns, I focus on evidence-based analysis that informs decision-making.

I believe in rigorous methodology, reproducible research, and clear communication. I'm passionate about using data to understand patterns in nature and inform evidence-based policy.
